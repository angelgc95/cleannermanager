## CleannerManager Cleaner Privacy Policy

CleannerManager Cleaner is a mobile application used by invited cleaning staff to manage operational tasks related to property turnovers, checklists, shopping requests, maintenance reports, work hours, and payout visibility.

### Information We Collect

We may collect:

- account information such as email address and profile details
- operational information such as assigned events, checklist submissions, shopping submissions, maintenance reports, and logged hours
- photos and notes uploaded by users as part of job completion or issue reporting

### How We Use Information

We use this information to:

- provide access to the app
- assign and manage cleaning work
- record completed operational tasks
- help hosts and cleaners coordinate supplies, maintenance, and hours
- maintain security, auditability, and service reliability

### Sharing

Information inside the platform may be visible to the host or organization that invited the cleaner, when required for operational use of the service.

We do not publish cleaner operational submissions publicly.

### Data Retention

We keep data for as long as needed to operate the service, support customer records, resolve disputes, and meet legal or contractual obligations.

### Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect service data. No method of storage or transmission is guaranteed to be completely secure.

### User Rights

Users may contact the service operator to request account support, corrections, or deletion requests where applicable.
